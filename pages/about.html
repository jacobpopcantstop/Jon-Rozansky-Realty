My Story

Experience Powered by Innovation

I've guided clients through three booms, two recessions, and the AI revolution.

When I started in San Diego real estate in 1996, the internet was barely a thing, and "AI" was science fiction. Fax machines were cutting-edge technology. Yet even then, I understood that real estate isn't about transactions—it's about transformation. It's about helping families write the next chapter of their lives.

Thirty years later, the tools have changed, but my philosophy hasn't. I've embraced every technological advancement—from MLS databases to virtual tours to AI-powered market analysis—not because they're trendy, but because they help me serve you better.

"My experience isn't just time served—it's wisdom earned through every market cycle, every economic shift, and thousands of families helped."

What truly sets me apart isn't the technology I use or even my years of experience. It's my commitment to one simple promise: When you hire me, you get me. Not a junior associate. Not a call center. Direct access to principal-level expertise, every single time.
