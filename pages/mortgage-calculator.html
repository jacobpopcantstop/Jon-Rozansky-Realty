Helpful Information

Understanding Your Mortgage

Key factors that affect your monthly payment and total cost.

Down Payment

A larger down payment reduces your loan amount and monthly payment. Putting 20% down also helps you avoid Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI).

Interest Rate

Even a small difference in interest rate can significantly impact your monthly payment and total cost over the life of the loan. Shop around for the best rates.

Loan Term

A 15-year mortgage has higher monthly payments but saves significantly on interest compared to a 30-year loan. Consider your budget and goals.

Property Taxes

San Diego property taxes average about 1% of the home's assessed value annually. Thanks to Prop 13, your assessed value increases are limited.

Home Insurance

Homeowner's insurance protects your investment. Costs vary based on location, home value, and coverage levels. Budget $1,500-$3,000 annually in San Diego.

HOA Fees

Many San Diego communities have HOA fees ranging from $100 to $500+ monthly. These cover amenities, maintenance, and community services.
